ABOUT US

ABOUT DE ATLAS SCHOOLS

Atlas Model School was established on 17th September, 2017, with the main mission to build every child's core values and nurture their dreams through sound basic and secondary foundation in academics as well as other extracurricular activities, without one taking over the other. This school was born to strike a balance between the grounds of academics, character building and capacity building, which are the founding blocks of every child's career path. At De Atlas Model School, we foster our values through a well thought process of inter and intra personal relationships, creativity, the virtues of self-discipline, tolerance, love, fairness, judgement, self-reliance, integrity and self discipline in our students right from the start. While we do not have a system of rigid rules, our overall Code of Conduct is based on the need to ensure the security of the students as well as consideration for others and high standards of behaviour. Our school has no formal links to any religious denomination, though the school has always acknowledged its Christian tradition.

VISION

Being a citadel of learning beyond the present, in an open and welcoming atmosphere that builds children towards being global citizens.

MISSION

Provision of Wholistic, inclusive, Qualitative Education for every child.

OUR STAND

The school is inclusive for all and sundry. It is an entity that embraces all without bias for tribe or religion, neither is there creation of social strata amongst students.
